Helpful Hearth

At Julia's, we're not just about threads – we're about spreading love, too! Partnering with a local charity, we groove together to fill empty tables for the homeless. Our commitment goes beyond just partnering; we organize a multitude of upcoming cloth and food drives, each an invitation to join the rhythm of giving. Check our calendar to sync up with the vibes of our next drive and be a part of the movement, where community and compassion collide in a symphony of generosity!

Greenwich Education Society

Julia and the G.E.S have been inseparable since the groovy days of 2016, vibing together to teach the young generation, aged 12 to 18, the true beauty of upcycling. We're not just mending clothes; we're preventing an excess of material waste and helping young adults build skills that will make them more independent. It's a journey of creativity, sustainability, and empowerment. Join us in stitching the fabric of a brighter future, one thread at a time!

Farley's Charity Corner

Step into a world where corner cafés, charity shops, and retro revolutions collide! Julia's latest team-up with Farley's isn't just a sponsorship; it's a groovy alliance brewing good vibes across Britain. With corner cafés as our stage and charity shops as our backstage, we're set to host the hottest event of mid-summer 2024 – the Groovy Greenwich Gala! If you're feeling the urge to be part of the action, to volunteer and help host the gala, slide into our contact form and let's make this retro rendezvous an unforgettable celebration of love and fashion!
