Fitting Appointments

For those seeking a truly tailored touch, consider our appointments at Julia's. Sign up for personalized tailoring and measuring sessions to ensure your vintage find fits like a glove. If you're yearning for a more hands-on experience, indulge in One on One workshop classes where our experts guide you through the creation of your bespoke piece. At Julia's, we believe in making fashion a personal journey, one stitch at a time.
Due to high demand, we now ask all customers to book all appointments in advance via our event booking form.

Dive into the groove with our Workshops, where you can unleash your creative side. Sign up for upcycling classes that breathe new life into old threads or join sewing workshops that transport you back to the era of handmade craftsmanship. Immerse yourself in the vintage spirit with other events that celebrate the art of self-expression and sustainable fashion. At Julia's, it's not just about the clothes; it's about the experience!
